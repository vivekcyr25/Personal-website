# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is integral to the Neural OS architecture. This policy outlines how we handle data within the platform.

## Data Collection

- **Authentication**: We use Firebase Auth (Google/GitHub) to manage your identity. We store your UID, email, and display name.
- **AI Interactions**: Prompts sent to the AI Core are processed via Groq Cloud. We may store session history locally or in Firestore for your convenience.
- **Analytics**: We collect basic platform usage metrics to optimize performance.

## Data Sovereignty

- Your data is yours. You can request deletion of your account and associated data through the Configuration dashboard.
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Security

- All data transmission is encrypted via SSL/TLS.
- We use industry-standard security protocols to protect your identity and sessions.

## Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact privacy@viveksharma.dev.
